**Role**
Senior Web Application Developer — individual contributor or

Early-stage startup · Growth-stage startup · Agency · Mid-size company

## How I Work

**Async-first** — I communicate clearly in writing, keep documentation
up to date, and don't need constant meetings to stay aligned.

**End-to-end ownership** — I take a feature from spec to production.
I ask the right questions upfront, flag problems early, and don't
drop the ball at handoff.

**Collaborative with product and design** — I've worked closely with
PMs and designers my whole career. I push back when something doesn't
make sense technically, but I do it constructively.

**Mentor by default** — I enjoy working with junior and mid-level
developers. I give honest code reviews, explain my reasoning, and
invest in the people around me.

**Strong technical writer** — I write READMEs, ADRs, runbooks, and
internal docs that people actually read. Good writing is part of
good engineering.

## What I'm NOT Looking For

- Fully on-site roles
- Roles where "senior" means glorified ticket-closer
- Environments that reward heroics over sustainable engineering
- Companies without any engineering culture or process
- Purely greenfield with no real users yet (unless the founding team is exceptional)
